~~HOW TO PLAY~~
THE KEYS A,D,W ARE USED TO CONTROL THE PLAYER.
THE ARROW KEYS UP,DOWN,LEFT,RIGHT ARE USED TO CONTROL THE DESTROYER OBJECT.

~~RULES~~
AVOID THE COLLISION BETWEEN THE DESTROYER OBJECT AND THE PLAYER AND TO KILL ZOMBIES MAKE A HIT ON ZOMBIES.
